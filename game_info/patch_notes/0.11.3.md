# Patch 0.11.3
> Massive visual + QOL changes!

## Features:
- When a unit hits their minimum cooldown, the cooldown number in the unit UI will be colored yellow instead of white
- Rank icons!
- Lots of new pretty UI (Profile / Leaderboard, Options, Game Modes, Library)

## Units:
- New art:
  - Time Dilutor
  - Power Plant
  - Bot Buffer
  - Atomic Timekeeper (previously Atomic Clock)
- New animations:
  - Weather Machine
  - Firepot Trebuchet
  - Wardens Flame
- Infinity Pool: No longer turns its target into Magic units
- Toy Turret: From 100 Health to 120 Health
- Powerline: From 10% cooldown decrease to 15%
- Velmir: Cooldown from 2.1s to 2.5s

## Bugs:
- EMP mine was still highlighting friendly units on hover
- Tutorial still used the old Quest UI
- Mecha Dragon could trigger itself with accelerate
- Atomic clock would speed up units when destroyed
- Research Facility, Taron the Enchanter and Post Office will now trigger **Start of Combat** Enchantments when they're given to a unit
- You could accidentally click "find opponent" right after pressing "continue" after a fight
- Extra shops could still spawn even if you don't have the mana for them after Replicator triggers (shop spawns now wait until all actions have resolved)

## Other:
- Fixed various small typos
- Small details added to other UI screens
- Hero shows up on end screen
