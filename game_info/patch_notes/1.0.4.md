# Patch 1.0.4
> Balance changes!

## Encounters
- Most big encounters now have lower chances of showing up

## Units:
- New art:
  - Jefferson
  - Bank
- Ancestral Keep: Nerfed effect to no longer gain any Damage on units summoned. Buffed base Damage from 20 -> 40
- Elder Dandelion: Nerfed tree summons when destroyed from 4 -> 2
- Elder Tiger Lily: Nerfed amount from 5 -> 6 nature units needing to be destroyed.
- EMP Mine: Nerfed cooldown increase from 0.2s -> 0.1s
- Altering Stone: Now needs at least 2 Health to trigger
- Efficio: Nerfed Damage buff from +2 -> +1
- Bell Ringer: Buffed cooldown from 5.5s -> 3.5s
- Warden's Flame: Buffed Damage gain from 15% -> 20%
- Cannon: Buffed Damage from 85 -> 90
- Infinity Pool: Buffed Health from 300 -> 330
- Blighted Ballista: Buffed triggers required from 20 -> 18
- Magnus: Buffed Damage from 50 -> 60
- Magic Missile Mine: Nerfed Explosive from 30% -> 20%
- Marketplace: Buffed cooldown from 1.5s -> 1s
- Spirit Torch: Nerfed Damage buff from 15% to 10%
- Cursed Bell: Buffed Health from 160 -> 200
- Weather Machine: Buffed cooldown from 3.5s -> 3s
- Obelisk: Buffed cooldown from 4s -> 3s
- Blind Priestess: Buffed cooldown buff from 10% -> 15% and health buff from 30% -> 35%
- Spirit Fisher: Buff extra spread from 10% -> 25%
- Weaponizer: Nerfed Damage from 40 -> 30
- Pyromancer: Buffed Damage from 3 -> 4

## Heroes:
- Zapzap Thriving Trees quest: Changed to require five Tier 1 Nature units instead of 4 Trees.

## Bugs:
- Powerplant could trigger on non-cooldown tinker units (mainly Weaponizer)
- The extra weapon encounters on round 3 and 5 did not yet check the inventory for weapons for the threshold

## Other:
- Gravekeeper info reworded for clarity
