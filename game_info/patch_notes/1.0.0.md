# Patch 1.0.0
> Early access release!

## Developer Update:
SkyShard has now entered Early Access! Today we're dropping a huge update in this patch with a ton of new units, solo adventures, art, features, and more. Throughout the rest of the year, we'll be adding even more content and putting the final touches on the game. You'll find a roadmap at the bottom of these patch notes with our future plans!   

## Features:
- Steam Achievements
- Steam Cloud Syncing
- Toggling island stats (info button bottom left) now also shows the enchantments on all units
- Island stats button now also appears when the game ends, so you can see the enchantments of your final build
- Matchmaking improvements to increase opponent variety
  - *The server now stores max 3 boards on each round for each user, which will guarantee that you face a wider range of boards even if some players play more at a given time during the day than others*
- New Solo Adventures:
  - The Wall
  - The Weaponsmith
  - Merus the Pure
  - The Fallen Archmage
  - The Grove
  - Ambrose
  - Auris
  - ZapZap (significantly updated)
- New Default island art
- New Ambrose island art
