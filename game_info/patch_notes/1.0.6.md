# Patch 1.0.6
> New units!

## Units:
- **New units**:
  - **Wizards' Relic** (Tier 1 Ancient)
  - **Mini-Mergus** (Tier 1 Novus)
  - **Siphoner** (Tier 3 Magic)
- **New art:**
  - **Elder Tiger Lily**
  - **Aequaliser**
  - **Excubitor**
  - **Furarium**
  - **Suppressor Rod**
- **Bee Keeper**: Wording changed to no longer be a *Start of Combat* unit.
- **Marketplace**: Reworked to a cooldown unit.
  - This rework is still subject to change, but I wanted something that already is more useful and strays away from the fatigue flavor.
- **Emergus**: Nerfed cooldown from *2s -> 2.5s*

## Bugs:
- **Pacifist**: The tier 1 ancient encounter would return no units, getting you soft-locked. Grand Canopy could also give you a Pyromancer or Toy Store. These are now all disabled for this mode.
- **Grave Keeper**: Stopped working, he's now back to creating his tombstones.
- **The Hole**: Even on the enemy board it would give the player extra mana.
