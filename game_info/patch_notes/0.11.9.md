# Patch 0.11.9
> More new art, UI and a few balance changes.

## Features:
- Updated UI:
  - Island Stats
  - Combat Stats
  - Inventory

## Units:
- Kudzu: Nerfed Summons from 5 times -> 3, Health buff from +2 -> +5 and Cooldown from 4s -> 3.5s
  - *Kudzu has been a tricky unit to balance, because its effect can become so distuptive. We've exeprimented with different approaches but are going to test out less summons right now.*
- Velmir the Wizard: Nerfed Explosive buff from +1% -> +0.5%
- Warden's Flame: Buffed Cooldown from 1.8s -> 1.5s
- Red Sun Shrine: Buffed Cooldown from 1.4s -> 1.3s
- Firepot Trebuchet: Nerfed Damage from 75 -> 70
- New art:
  - Infinity Pool
  - Spirit Torch
  - Derelict (previously Ritualist)

## Heroes:
- Zapzap: polished art!!

## Bugs:
- Right-clicking on the enchantment encounter didn't filter the library properly
- Kudzu's final spawns kept their cooldown instead of stopping after 1 trigger
- You could open a shop right after clicking *find opponent* and get stuck

## Other:
- Second Chance Enchantment: no longer spreads to summoned units during combat
